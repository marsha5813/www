CRCS interview series

Every month (or most months), I produce and host an audio or video interview for my research center, Purdue's Center on Religion and Chinese Society. These interviews are usually brief (~15-30 min) and are intended for a broad audience. Guests are people who have made an interesting contribution to research on religion in China. The following YouTube playlist contains some of my favorites. Click the menu bar in the upper-left corner of the player for more. If you have trouble with the player, you can visit the media page of the Center on Religion and Chinese Society or just check out one of my favorite video productions for an example.
